Cotton Plant Disease Prediction using AI

AI technologies help farmers optimize planning to generate more bountiful yields by determining crop choices, diseases effected and the best solution for it. AI systems are also helping to improve harvest quality and accuracy -- what is known as precision agriculture. Precision agriculture uses AI technology to aid in detecting diseases in plants, pests, and poor plant nutrition on farms.

Plant pathogens can vary from fungal, bacterial, viral or nematodes

Plant pathogens can be fungal, bacterial, viral or nematodes and can damage plant parts. Identifying symptoms and knowing when and how to effectively control diseases is crucial. In order to detect and diagnose we will be using Deep learning is best way for detecting and diagnosis of the disease. Using CNN model we predict whether it is diseased or not.

Smart way to effectively identify diseases at early stage

In the agricultural field, loss of yield mainly occurs due to widespread of disease. Identifying diseases through naked eye is often prone to high error rates and faulty classification. Development of automatic detection system using advanced computer technology such as Image Processing help to support farmers in the identification of disease at an initial stage and provide useful information for its control.
